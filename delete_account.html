Request Account & Data Deletion

At XD Developer, we prioritize your privacy. Because our apps (XDFamily, KomKal, ReadComp) are local-first, most of your data is stored directly on your device.

How to Delete Your Data:

1. In-App Deletion (Recommended): Open the app settings and select "Clear All Data" or "Delete Profile." This immediately removes all genealogy, portraits, and progress from your device.

2. Cloud/Account Deletion: If you created an account for syncing or beta testing, please email us to request a manual wipe of any data associated with your email address.

Email: kenn.wanaku@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - [App Name]

Requests are typically processed within 48-72 hours. Once deleted, genealogy data cannot be recovered unless you have a separate JSON backup.
