## Child Safety Contact: kenn.wanaku@gmail.com

XDFamily was built from the ground up to ensure your data stays where it belongs: on your device.

* **Local-First Storage**: All family tree data, including names, relationships, and notes, is stored strictly on your local device's internal database.
* **Photo Privacy**: When you add a family portrait, the app saves a copy to its local internal storage. We do not transmit, upload, or share your photos with any external servers.
* **No Data Collection**: We do not use third-party tracking or analytics. We have no access to your family tree data.
* **Data Portability**: You own your data. You can export your entire tree to a JSON file for your own backups at any time.
